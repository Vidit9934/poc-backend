id: sop-billing-002
title: General billing inquiry SOP
---

When a customer has a general billing question (invoice clarification, charge explanation, payment failure):

1. Identify the specific billing concern from the ticket subject and body.
2. For invoice clarification: explain each line item clearly using plain language.
3. For payment failures: advise the customer to check their card details and retry; offer to resend the payment link if applicable.
4. For unexpected charges: confirm the charge is legitimate before explaining it; if it cannot be confirmed, escalate to billing team (Phase 1 — for POC, acknowledge and note investigation).
5. Never quote specific amounts or account balances unless the SOP or ticket gives them explicitly.

**Tone:** Neutral and factual, but warm.

**Do not:** Promise refunds without following sop-refund-001.
