title: General inquiry and catch-all SOP
---

When a customer's ticket does not clearly match a specific category (billing, account, shipping, technical):

1. Acknowledge the customer's question or concern in the first sentence.
2. If the topic can be partially answered, provide what information is available.
3. If further investigation is needed, set the expectation: "Our team will look into this and follow up within 1 business day."
4. Do not make promises about outcomes you cannot guarantee.
5. Close warmly and invite the customer to reply if they have additional questions.

**Tone:** Professional and warm. Uncertainty should feel reassuring, not dismissive.

**Use this SOP when:**
- The request is ambiguous.
- No other SOP clearly applies.
- The ticket is a general question or compliment.
