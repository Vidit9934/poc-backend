title: Duplicate charge refund SOP
---

When a customer reports a duplicate charge:

1. Acknowledge the duplicate charge in the opening line.
2. Verify the duplicate in the billing system (mock for POC: accept at face value).
3. Process a refund for the duplicate amount within 5 business days.
4. Confirm the refund timeline explicitly in the reply.
5. If the customer is unsure whether it is a duplicate, ask them to provide the invoice numbers for both charges.

**Tone:** Professional and apologetic. Acknowledge the inconvenience.

**Example reply opening:**
"Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We've identified the duplicate charge on your account and have initiated a refund, which will reflect within 5 business days."
