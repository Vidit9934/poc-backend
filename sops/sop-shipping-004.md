title: Shipping and delivery issues SOP
---

When a customer has a shipping or delivery concern (delayed, lost, wrong item, damaged):

1. Delayed orders: check if the order is past the estimated delivery window. If yes, advise the customer to allow 2 additional business days before escalating, and provide the tracking link format.
2. Lost in transit: if tracking shows no movement for 5+ business days, initiate a resend or refund — confirm with the customer which they prefer.
3. Wrong item received: apologise, arrange return label, ship correct item within 3 business days of return confirmation.
4. Damaged item: apologise, request a photo of the damage (Phase 1 — for POC, accept the customer's description), arrange replacement or refund per customer preference.

**Tone:** Apologetic and action-oriented. Give clear next steps.

**Do not:** Promise delivery dates unless the SOP or the shipping carrier's tracking data provides one.
