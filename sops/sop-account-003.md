title: Account access and login issues SOP
---

When a customer cannot access their account (forgotten password, locked account, 2FA issues):

1. Direct password reset: instruct the customer to use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
2. Locked accounts (too many failed attempts): inform that the lock lifts automatically after 30 minutes, or support can unlock manually upon identity verification.
3. 2FA issues: if the customer has lost access to their authenticator, initiate recovery via backup email — ask them to check their registered email inbox for the recovery link.
4. Compromised accounts (customer suspects unauthorised access): instruct them to reset their password immediately and enable 2FA; flag for security review (Phase 1 escalation — for POC, note in reply).

**Tone:** Calm and reassuring. Account access issues cause anxiety.

**Do not:** Ask for or repeat passwords, tokens, or full card numbers in the reply.
